About

Caribbean Bali Villas represents a unique fusion of two of the world's most beloved tropical destinations. Nestled on the stunning west side of Puerto Rico, our property combines the serene, spiritual essence of Balinese architecture with the vibrant energy and natural beauty of the Caribbean.


Located on Puerto Rico's west coast, our villa offers guests easy access to some of the island's most spectacular attractions such as Crash Boat beach and Rincon local markets.


We are excited to share that Caribbean Bali Villas is currently under construction, with meticulous attention to detail. Our team of skilled craftsmen and designers are working to create a truly exceptional retreat that honors both Balinese traditions and Caribbean hospitality
